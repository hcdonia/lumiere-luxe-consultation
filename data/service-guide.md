# Lumiere Luxe Salon — New Guest Booking Guide

Three new guest main sessions. All sessions include a haircut, trim, or treatment.

---

## Quick Decision Matrix

| Guest situation / what she says | Recommended session |
|---|---|
| Wants bold sun-kissed blonde with dimension | The New Luxe |
| Brunette/bronde wanting subtle ribbons of color | The Natural Luxe |
| First-timer, teen, or wants minimal brightness | The Mini Luxe |
| Over-highlighted, needs to tone down | The Mini Luxe |
| Wants to stay brunette but add life and movement | The Natural Luxe |
| Loves babylights but not the time commitment | The New Luxe |

---

## Service: The New Luxe

- **Category:** Blonde / Lived-in
- **Key:** new-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut & style
- **Duration:** 2.5–4 hours
- **Price:** $352–$550

### Who This Is For
- Wants to be noticeably blonde with dimension — not flat, not all-over solid
- Loves babylights but doesn't want to sit for 5+ hours
- Has some existing lightness or is naturally medium-to-light brown
- Wants a sun-kissed, effortless result that grows out seamlessly
- Has done highlights or balayage before and wants to take it up a notch
- Values low maintenance — doesn't want to be back in 6 weeks

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants solid, all-over light blonde with no dimension → The Mini Luxe or consult
- Wants only a subtle change or is a first-timer → The Mini Luxe
- Wants to stay brunette with very soft contrast → The Natural Luxe
- Starting from very dark, heavily processed, or box-colored hair → flag for consultation

### Phrases She Uses
"sun-kissed", "bright but natural", "seamless grow-out", "beachy blonde", "dimensional", "babylights but faster"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 10–16 weeks. Low-maintenance by design — grows out beautifully with no harsh lines.

---

## Service: The Natural Luxe

- **Category:** Brunette / Bronde
- **Key:** natural-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut & style
- **Duration:** 2–3.5 hours
- **Price:** $275–$495

### Who This Is For
- Happy with her base color but wants hair to look more alive and dimensional
- Brunette, bronde, or soft blonde wanting ribbons of contrast — not a color change
- Wants to enhance her natural tone, not dramatically lighten it
- Prioritizes a seamless grow-out and low-maintenance results above all else
- Wants to keep her natural root growth and add pop throughout the lengths

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants to go significantly lighter or become noticeably blonde → The New Luxe
- Only wants a minimal touch of brightness → The Mini Luxe
- Wants solid all-over color → consult required
- Has significant gray she's trying to address → consult required

### Phrases She Uses
"I don't want to go lighter", "more life in my hair", "keep my brown", "effortless", "natural but dimensional"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 12–18 weeks. The most grow-out-friendly service. Mini sessions and the Lived-in Partial are available for a quick refresh around week 12.

---

## Service: The Mini Luxe

- **Category:** Entry-level / Subtle
- **Key:** mini-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut & style
- **Duration:** 1.5–2.5 hours
- **Price:** $190–$319

### Who This Is For
- Getting highlights for the very first time — wants something simple and easy
- Teens dipping their toes into lived-in color for the first time
- Wants a soft money piece or face frame — nothing all over
- Currently over-highlighted and wants to tone it down for a more polished, natural result
- Wants a sun-kissed glow with 8–12 strategic foils — nothing dramatic

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants significant all-over brightness or dimension → The New Luxe
- Wants dimensional contrast throughout brunette hair → The Natural Luxe
- Has significant gray to address → consult required
- Expects a dramatic transformation — this is a subtle, strategic service

### Phrases She Uses
"nothing too crazy", "just a little brightness", "first time", "face frame", "money piece", "I'm over-highlighted"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 12–20 weeks. Minimal placements mean very gradual grow-out — a great entry point before stepping into a larger session.
