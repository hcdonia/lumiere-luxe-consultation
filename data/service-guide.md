# Lumiere Luxe Salon — New Guest Booking Guide

All five new guest main sessions. All sessions include a haircut, trim, or treatment.

---

## Quick Decision Matrix

| Guest situation / what she says | Recommended session |
|---|---|
| Wants all-over uniform lightening | Platinum Blonding |
| Wants bold sun-kissed blonde with dimension | The New Luxe |
| Brunette/bronde wanting subtle ribbons of color | The Natural Luxe |
| First-timer, teen, or wants minimal brightness | The Mini Luxe |
| Wants to embrace gray, leave root touch-ups behind | Gray Blending Transformation |
| Over-highlighted, needs to tone down | The Mini Luxe |

---

## Service: The New Luxe

- **Category:** Blonde / Lived-in
- **Key:** new-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut
- **Duration:** 2.5–4 hours
- **Price:** $352–$550

### Who This Is For
- Wants to be noticeably blonde with dimension — not flat, not all-over solid
- Loves babylights but doesn't want to sit for 5+ hours
- Has some existing lightness or is naturally medium-to-light brown
- Wants a sun-kissed, effortless result that grows out seamlessly
- Has done highlights or balayage before and wants to take it up a notch
- Values low maintenance — doesn't want to be back in 6 weeks

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants solid, all-over light blonde with no dimension → Platinum Blonding
- Wants only a subtle change or is a first-timer → The Mini Luxe
- Wants to stay brunette with very soft contrast → The Natural Luxe
- Starting from very dark, heavily processed, or box-colored hair → flag for consultation

### Phrases She Uses
"sun-kissed", "bright but natural", "seamless grow-out", "beachy blonde", "dimensional", "babylights but faster"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 10–16 weeks. Low-maintenance by design — grows out beautifully with no harsh lines.

---

## Service: The Natural Luxe

- **Category:** Brunette / Bronde
- **Key:** natural-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut
- **Duration:** 2–3.5 hours
- **Price:** $275–$495

### Who This Is For
- Happy with her base color but wants hair to look more alive and dimensional
- Brunette, bronde, or soft blonde wanting ribbons of contrast — not a color change
- Wants to enhance her natural tone, not dramatically lighten it
- Prioritizes a seamless grow-out and low-maintenance results above all else
- Wants to keep her natural root growth and add pop throughout the lengths

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants to go significantly lighter or become noticeably blonde → The New Luxe
- Only wants a minimal touch of brightness → The Mini Luxe
- Wants solid all-over color → Platinum Blonding
- Has significant gray she's trying to address → Gray Blending Transformation

### Phrases She Uses
"I don't want to go lighter", "more life in my hair", "keep my brown", "effortless", "natural but dimensional"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 12–18 weeks. The most grow-out-friendly service. Mini sessions and the Lived-in Partial are available for a quick refresh around week 12.

---

## Service: The Mini Luxe

- **Category:** Entry-level / Subtle
- **Key:** mini-luxe
- **Includes:** Haircut
- **Duration:** 1.5–2.5 hours
- **Price:** $190–$319

### Who This Is For
- Getting highlights for the very first time — wants something simple and easy
- Teens dipping their toes into lived-in color for the first time
- Wants a soft money piece or face frame — nothing all over
- Currently over-highlighted and wants to tone it down for a more polished, natural result
- Wants a sun-kissed glow with 8–12 strategic foils — nothing dramatic

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants significant all-over brightness or dimension → The New Luxe
- Wants dimensional contrast throughout brunette hair → The Natural Luxe
- Wants all-over lightening → Platinum Blonding
- Has significant gray to address → Gray Blending Transformation

### Phrases She Uses
"nothing too crazy", "just a little brightness", "first time", "face frame", "money piece", "I'm over-highlighted"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 12–20 weeks. Minimal placements mean very gradual grow-out — a great entry point before stepping into a larger session.

---

## Service: Platinum Blonding Transformation

- **Category:** All-over / High Maintenance
- **Key:** platinum-blonding
- **Includes:** Haircut
- **Duration:** 2 hour minimum
- **Price:** $125/hour (from $250)

### Who This Is For
- Wants to lighten their entire head of hair for a uniform, all-over lighter appearance
- Has a history of foiling but is ready to move into a full color result — not a lived-in look
- Fully committed to a high-maintenance schedule — comfortable returning every 4–10 weeks
- Wants the lightest, most uniform result possible — no dimension, no lived-in softness

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants brightness with dimension or a soft grow-out → The New Luxe
- Not prepared for or committed to a 4–10 week return schedule
- Has severely damaged, over-processed, or box-colored hair → consultation required first
- Starting from very dark hair without understanding this may be a multi-session process

### Phrases She Uses
"all over blonde", "platinum", "I want to be fully light", "icy", "I hate seeing any dark", "white blonde", "no dimension"

### Maintenance Expectation
Every 4–10 weeks — highest maintenance service offered. Guest must fully understand this commitment before booking.

### Pricing & Timing
$125/hour — 2 hour minimum ($250+). Final timing and cost depend on the guest's existing hair. Consultation is required before booking to assess.

### AI Note
Always confirm the guest understands the maintenance commitment. If she seems hesitant about that commitment, redirect her to The New Luxe, which delivers a bright, beautiful result with less upkeep.

## Service: Gray Blending Transformation

- **Category:** Gray Transition
- **Key:** gray-blending
- **Includes:** Haircut
- **Duration:** 2 hour minimum
- **Price:** $125/hour (from $250)

### Who This Is For
- Ready to lean into her gray and make it more noticeable — not hide it
- Wants to transition out of her every 3–6 week root touch-up routine
- Tired of being tied to frequent salon visits just to maintain solid color coverage
- Understands this is a multi-step journey — not a single-appointment fix
- Emotionally prepared to see her gray integrate gradually and trusts the process
- Values long-term low maintenance and is willing to go through the transition phase

### Who This Is NOT For
- Wants to fully cover or hide gray → different root coverage service
- Only slightly gray with no real concern → The Natural Luxe may be more appropriate
- Not emotionally ready to see gray grow in — she must be prepared for the transition
- Expects a finished result after one appointment — this is a multi-visit transformation

### Phrases She Uses
"I want to go gray", "tired of root touch-ups", "going natural", "silver fox", "embrace my gray", "sick of coming in every month"

### Maintenance Expectation
Multi-visit transformation. Long-term low maintenance once the transition is complete.

### Pricing & Timing
$125/hour — 2 hour minimum ($250+). Final timing and cost depend on the guest's existing hair. Consultation is required before booking.

### AI Note
Consultation is essential. Guest must be assessed for current color, percentage of gray, overall hair health, skin tone, desired appearance, and realistic timelines. This is one of the most complex services — it may involve removing pigment from previously colored hair, redistributing natural gray patterns, and precision toning. If she is not emotionally ready to see gray grow in, this service is not for her right now.
